How this is calculated (assumptions)
  • Earnings are modelled from published 2025 pilot ranges: households with V2G earned a median of roughly USD 420 to 780 per year from grid services and bill savings.
  • A regional multiplier scales that baseline up or down by how developed each market's V2G rules and tariffs are.
  • Earnings also scale gently with battery size and participation days, with diminishing returns, since real programmes cap how much they call on any one vehicle.
  • Utilization reflects the finding that even active V2G participants discharge to the grid under 2 percent of the year, fewer than 200 hours per vehicle.
  • Figures are illustrative estimates for discussion, not financial advice. Real results depend on your utility, tariff, vehicle, and local interconnection rules.
